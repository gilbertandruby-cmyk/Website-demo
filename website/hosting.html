Frequently Asked Questions

Is Elvis Bot free?

Yes! Elvis Bot has a free tier with 1 bot instance. Upgrade to Pro for more features.

Yes! Free users get community support. Pro & Enterprise get priority support.

Absolutely! Elvis Bot is fully open-source and highly customizable.
