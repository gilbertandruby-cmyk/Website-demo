Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Pair Code?
A pair code is a unique 9-digit code that links your bot to your WhatsApp account. It's more secure than QR codes and works on any device.
Is my account safe?
Yes! Your account remains under your control. The bot acts as a linked device, just like WhatsApp Web. You can disconnect anytime.
What if the code expires?
If your code expires, simply generate a new one. You have 5 minutes to use the code after generation.
Can I use multiple devices?
Yes! You can link up to 5 devices simultaneously with your WhatsApp account.
